HTML Lists & Tables Demonstration

Unordered List (<ul>)

Ma'am Yeh "Unordered List" (ul) hai. Yeh bullet points istemaal karti hai jab order zaroori nahi hota. Jaise, ek simple grocery list:

  • Apples
  • Milk
  • Bread
  • Eggs

Ordered List (<ol>)

Yeh "Ordered List" (ol) hai. Yeh numbers (1, 2, 3) istemaal karti hai jab steps ya order zaroori hota hai. Jaise, chai banane ke steps:

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add tea leaves and sugar.
  3. Add milk.
  4. Pour into cup and serve.

The Table Tag (<table>)

Yeh "Table" (table) (thead,tr,th,td) hai. Yeh data ko rows aur columns me organize karne ke liye hota hai. Jaise, kuch famous AI Models ki summary jo mere interest ka topic hain:

ChatGPT (GPT-5) OpenAI Great all-rounder, strong in conversation & coding.
Gemini (Pro/Ultra) Google Natively multi-modal (understands text, images, audio).
Claude 4.5 (Sonnet) Anthropic Excellent at handling large documents & better at reasoning coding tasks.
DeepSeek Coder DeepSeek AI A specialized model trained for writing code (like Python!).
